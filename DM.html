About DC Driver


In an innovative effort to combat the rising issue of distracted driving among young motorists, students of the 2024 Class of Howard University Computer Science have developed a groundbreaking mobile application available on both iOS and Android platforms. This app harnesses cutting-edge technology to encourage safer driving habits, directly addressing the challenges and dangers of distracted driving. Through user-friendly interfaces and intelligent features, the application provides real-time feedback, alerts, and educational content designed to engage drivers in a meaningful way. It employs sensors and data analytics to monitor driving behavior, offering personalized suggestions and rewards for improved driving practices. This project not only showcases technical prowess and commitment to social responsibility but also represents a significant step forward in enhancing road safety for young drivers and their communities.
